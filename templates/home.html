Simple by design

From clone to production in three steps

  1. 1

    Clone & configure

    Clone the repo, copy .env.example, fill in your Stripe and email credentials. Takes 5 minutes.

  2. 2

    Build your product

    Add your own Django apps inside apps/. The boilerplate handles auth, billing, and teams so you focus on your unique value.

  3. 3

    Deploy to AWS

    Run terraform apply to spin up ECS Fargate, RDS, ElastiCache, ALB, and S3 in one command.
